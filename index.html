Aluminum vs PVC: Which is Right for You?

Aluminum Benefits

  • Exceptional durability and strength
  • Modern, sleek appearance
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Excellent for large window spans
  • 100% recyclable material

PVC Benefits

  • Superior thermal insulation
  • Cost-effective solution
  • Excellent noise reduction
  • Wide range of colors and finishes
  • Resistant to corrosion and weathering
Feature Aluminum PVC
Durability Excellent (30+ years) Good (20-25 years)
Thermal Insulation Good (with thermal break) Excellent
Maintenance Low Very Low
Cost Higher initial cost More affordable
Best For Modern designs, large spans Energy efficiency, budget
